The Perfect Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

There is no sandwich quite like the peanut butter and jelly. PB&Js are staples within the average American lunchbox, oft paired with a carton of milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peter Pan peanut butter spread
  • Jelly or marmalade of your choice

Steps

  1. Place two pieces of bread on a cutting board.
  2. Apply peanut butter to one slice of bread.
  3. Coat the other slice of bread in the jelly or marmalade of your choice.
  4. Join the two pieces of bread and slice diagonally. Serve with a side of chips and a tall glass of milk.
