The Quintessential Latte

Description

Making the perfect latte requires a great blend of espresso beans, mad milk frothing skills, and an expensive espresso machine.

Ingredients

  • espresso beans
  • whole milk
  • sweetener of your choice

Steps

  1. Grind the espresso beans and tamp them so they are compact.
  2. Brew a shot of espresso into a cup.
  3. Use the milk wand to froth milk in a seperate cup.
  4. To create latte art, pour in a zig-zag pattern and pull upward. This should make a nice floret. Sweeten with sugar, simple syrup, or agave nectar.
